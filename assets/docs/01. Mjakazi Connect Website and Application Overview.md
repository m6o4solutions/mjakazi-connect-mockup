## Overview

Mjakazi Connect Limited aims to build a digital platform that connects families with reliable caregivers and domestic staff. The goal is to make it easier for households to discover, evaluate, and engage caregivers, while giving job seekers a credible channel to present themselves and access opportunities.

The platform will function as both a public website and a restricted-access web application. It focuses on visibility, trust, and controlled access rather than managing the full employment lifecycle.

---

## 1\. Public marketing website

### What is built

* A public-facing website that explains the purpose of Mjakazi Connect  
    
* Clear positioning as a platform for connecting families with caregivers and domestic staff  
    
* Distinct entry points for:  
    
  * Families looking for caregivers  
  * Caregivers seeking work

### Why it is necessary

* Users need to understand the value before registering  
* Families require reassurance before engaging with domestic staffing  
* Caregivers need confidence that the platform is legitimate and worth joining

---

## 2\. Caregiver listing directory

### What is built

* A searchable directory of caregiver profiles  
    
* Each profile includes:  
    
  * Profile photo  
  * Experience summary  
  * Skills and services offered  
  * Availability  
  * Location or service area

### Why it is necessary

* Families want choice and the ability to compare options  
* Browsing profiles builds confidence and a sense of control  
* The directory is the core value of the platform

---

## 3\. Caregiver profile pages

### What is built

* Individual profile pages for each caregiver  
* Limited information visible publicly  
* Full contact details restricted to registered or paying users

### Why it is necessary

* Families need sufficient detail to evaluate suitability  
* Access control protects caregiver privacy  
* Gated information supports monetization

## 4\. Family registration and access control

### What is built

* A registration process for families  
* Account-based access to restricted caregiver information  
* Optional payment or subscription to unlock full access

### Why it is necessary

* Prevents misuse of caregiver information  
* Converts interest into committed users  
* Enables tracking of serious inquiries
