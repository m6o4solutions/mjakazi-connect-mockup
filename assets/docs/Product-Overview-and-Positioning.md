## **1\. What are we building?**

**Web Application**

This is a functional, rule-driven product with user accounts, restricted access, subscriptions, administrative moderation, and ongoing user activity. It goes beyond static content and requires system-level behavior and enforcement.

---

## **2\. What does Mjakazi Connect do?**

Mjakazi Connect is a digital platform that connects families with domestic caregivers by providing a curated directory of caregiver profiles, controlled access to contact information through subscription, and job posting capabilities, all governed by active administrative oversight.

In simple terms:

- Families discover and evaluate contractors.
- Contractors present their skills and availability.
- The platform controls access, quality, and trust.

---

## **3\. Who are the primary users / target audience?**

**Primary users**

- Families and households looking for caregivers and domestic staff.

**Secondary users**

- Contractors and domestic workers seeking visibility and job opportunities.

**Internal users**

- Platform administrators responsible for moderation, approvals, and quality control.

---

## **4\. What is the main success metric?**

**Primary success metric**

- The number of paying families who actively unlock caregiver contact details or post jobs.

This measures real value creation by focusing on committed usage rather than surface-level engagement.

---

## **5\. Which “vibe” fits best?**

**SaaS / Tech**

This vibe supports:

- Trust and credibility
- Clear rules and boundaries
- Predictable, structured user experiences

**Secondary influence**

- Corporate / Enterprise, to reinforce seriousness and authority.

---

## **Final synthesis**

Mjakazi Connect is a subscription-based caregiver discovery platform designed to operate as a controlled, trustworthy environment rather than an open marketplace. It balances public visibility with restricted access, prioritizes quality through active moderation, and focuses on enabling meaningful connections between families and caregivers.

The product should feel reliable, structured, and deliberate, reinforcing confidence at every interaction. Its success depends on trust, clarity, and consistent enforcement of rules, not on novelty or experimentation.
